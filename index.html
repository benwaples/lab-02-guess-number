Ticket to Mars

Guess the number on your ticket and you can keep it and go to Mars for free!

instructions:

  1. click the button "TICKET" to get a ticket with a random number

  3. don't worry, we will let you know if your guess is too high or too low compared to the hidden number
  4. oh yeah, we are also keeping track of how many times you win... and lose!!! GOOD LUCK!!!!
